Bellini

    What's Needed

    Ingredients

  • 100 ml (2 parts) dry sparkling wine
  • 50 ml (1 part) fresh peach purée
  • Ingredient 1

    • Glassware

  • Champagne flute

    • Served

  • straight up without ice

What to Do

Pour peach puree into chilled flute, add gently sparkling wine. Stir gently.

Traditionally a Bellini uses Prosecco for the wine and white peaches for the fruit.
