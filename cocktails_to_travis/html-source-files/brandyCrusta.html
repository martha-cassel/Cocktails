Brandy Crusta

    What's Needed

    Ingredients

  • 2 oz. Cognac
  • 1 tsp. orange curaçao
  • 1/2 tsp. fresh lemon juice
  • 1 dash Angostura bitters
  • Crushed ice

    • Tools & Utensils

  • shaker
  • strainer
  • Utensils
  • Utensils

    • Glassware

  • cocktail (classic serving)

    • Garnish

  • sugar rim and lemon curl

What to Do

Cut a lemon in half. Pare the full peel off half and squize the juice from the lemon. Prepare glass by moistening the rim with lemon and dipping it in sugar, then carefully curling the lemon peel around the inside of the glass. Combine all ingredients in a shaker, shake and strain into glass and add 1 small cube of ice.

This classic cocktail was originally made with Cognac but can also be made with brandy, bourbon or rye whiskey. It's also one of the few cocktails that you garnish before making the drink.

Adapted from Vintage Spirits & Forgotten Cocktails by Ted Haigh
