Negroni

    What's Needed

    Ingredients

  • 30ml (1 part) gin
  • 30ml (1 part) sweet red vermouth
  • 30ml (1 part) campari

    • Tools & Utensils

  • none

    • Glassware

  • old fashioned, classic on the rocks
  • cocktail, straight up

    • Garnish

  • orange peel

What to Do

Stir into glass over ice, garnish and serve.
Served on the rocks over ice: simple, no stirring.


Primary alcohol by volume

gin, vermouth, Campari
