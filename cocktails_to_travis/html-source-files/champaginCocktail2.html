Champagne Cocktail

    What's Needed

    Ingredients

  • Champagne
  • 30ml Brandy
  • 1 sugar cube
  • 2-3 dashes Angostura bitters

    • Tools (straight)

  • mixing glass w/ stirrer
  • strainer

    • Glassware

  • Champagne Flute
  • old fashioned (rocks)

    • Standard Garnish

  • orange slice
  • marachino cherry

    • Served

  • straight or
  • on the rocks

What to Do

Place the sugar cube at the bottom of the flute and add Angostura bitters until it dissolves. Add the brandy, then fill with the champagne. Garnish with the orange slice and maraschino cherry.
